# Product Requirements Document - School ERP

## 1. Product Vision

To build a complete school automation system covering admission to payroll.

---

## 2. Target Users

- School Administrators
- Teachers
- Accountants
- HR
- Students
- Parents

---

## 3. Key Features

### Admission
- Online application
- Entrance exam
- Merit list
- Direct admission
- Auto ID generation

### Attendance
- Biometric
- Lecture-wise manual

### Exams
- MCQ auto grading
- Result generation
- Report card PDF

### Finance
- Fee management
- Payroll
- Accounts ledger

### Governance
- Role-based access
- Audit logs
- Backup system

---

## 4. Success Metrics

- Reduce admin workload by 60%
- Reduce fee leakage
- Reduce attendance fraud
- Automate payroll fully
